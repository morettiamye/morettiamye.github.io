About Me

My name is Amy. I'm a developer, with a love for CSS and all things style sheets. Originally from Tennessee, I've lived in Chicago by way of New Jersey for the last 8 years with my wonderful husband and our two cats.

I started out designing and building websites for friends back in the early 2000s, when in-line scrolling frames were all the rage and we built our designs in Paintshop Pro. Fast forward about 15 years, I've now had a career as a flight attendant and spent a considerable amount of time in specialty coffee. But web development was always in the back of my mind.

My back end developer husband helped me reignite my passion for web developmen and design, leading me to enroll at the Northwestern University Web Development Bootcamp. I've brushed up on HTML and CSS, and started learning Javascript. I've learned that I love learning new development languages, and building beautiful things. Now I'm excited to see where development can take me.

When I'm not developing or perusing the internet, I enjoy things like playing video games, catching all the Pokemon, and shopping for animation cels to add to my ever growing collection.
