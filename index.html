My name is Amy. I'm a front end web developer, with an unhealthy love for CSS and all things style sheets. I started out designing and building websites for friends back in the early 2000s, when in-line scrolling frames were all the rage and we built our designs in Paintshop Pro.

Fast forward about 15 years, I've now had a career as a flight attendant and spent a considerable amount of time in specialty coffee . I guess you could say I love traveling and good coffee.

My back end developer husband helped me reignite my passion for web development and design, leading me to enroll at the Northwestern University Web Dev Bootcamp. I've brushed up on HTML and CSS, and stuck my toes in the JavaScript water. Now I'm excited to see where web development can take me.
