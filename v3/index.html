Bio

I started out designing and building websites for friends back in the early 2000s, when in-line scrolling frames were all the rage and we built our designs in Paintshop Pro. More than 15 years later, I've now had a career as a flight attendant and spent a considerable amount of time in specialty coffee, but always felt myself missing web development. In the summer of 2018 I decided to leave my management position at a local coffee roaster to pursue development, and attended the Northwestern University Full Stack Coding Bootcamp to bring my skills up to date. Since then I have learned to love JavaScript and CSS, and prefer to focus on creating efficient, responsive, and accessible websites.

My software developer husband Tony and I live in the Edgewater neighborhood of Chicago along with our two cats Eloise and Fruff. When not working on web development, I can be found drinking lattes, playing video games, and cross stitching. I am also an avid collector of animation cels from my two favorite magical girl animes, Sailor Moon and Cardcaptor Sakura. This hobby has led me to spending many late nights and early mornings bidding against others on Yahoo! Japan Auctions.
